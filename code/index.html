The architect

Le Corbusier was a Swiss-French architect, designer, painter, urban planner, writer, and one of the pioneers of what is now regarded as modern architecture.

  • The National Museum of Western Art in Tokyo (1954–1959).
  • Carpenter Center, for the Visual Arts in Cambridge, Massachusetts (1960–1963).
  • Pavillon Le Corbusier in Zürich (1962–1967).
